我是郝飞洋，现就读于西安交通大学软件学院，欢迎访问我的主页。
Hello, welcome to my personal website! I'm Hao Feiyang, a student studying software engineering in Xi'an Jiaotong University.

我很喜欢乔布斯在演讲中提到的一句话——“虚心若愚，求知若渴。”人生是旷野，我会努力去探索，去感受。
"Stay hungry, stay foolish." I have hunger for adventure, and I think learning is a journey of a lifetime.

最近我正沉迷于深度学习相关的探索，模型的设计和调整都仿佛蕴藏着哲理。正如“真理”，我们需要通过不断实践来从不同的角度认识它们。
Recently, I've been obsessed with deep learning, and the design and tuning of models seem to be philosophical. As with "truths", we need to practice them to recognize them from different perspectives.
